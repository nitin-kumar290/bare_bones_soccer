About Bare Bones Soccer

Embarking on a mission to elevate the soccer community in the Greater Denver area, our goal is to establish ourselves as the ultimate destination for soccer enthusiasts. We envision becoming the go-to spot for engaging soccer gatherings and delightful, social games. Through strategic partnerships and collaborations with local soccer initiatives, we're committed to unifying the Denver soccer scene, fostering a sense of community and creating lasting connections on and off the field. Join us in shaping a more collective and vibrant soccer experience in Denver!

Who are we?

Bare Bones Soccer Collective is a community-driven soccer organization based in the Greater Denver Area. Committed to fostering a love for the sport, the collective organizes free pick-up soccer games and social events. With a focus on inclusivity and building connections within the community, Bare Bones Soccer Collective creates a welcoming space for soccer enthusiasts of all skill levels to come together, play and enjoy the beautiful game in a friendly & social environment.
